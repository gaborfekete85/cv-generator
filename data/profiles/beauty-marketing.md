# Long-form background

Beauty marketer who grew up at the intersection of performance and brand.
I love the tension between the creative that makes someone feel something
on a Tuesday night scroll and the spreadsheet the next morning that proves
the campaign worked.

Eight years in skincare and cosmetics have taught me that the best beauty
brands obsess over two things: the craft of the product and the honesty of
the community around it. My job is to let both show up in the marketing —
whether that's a TikTok unboxing by a micro-influencer or a 30-page brand
book for a Sephora negotiation.
