Paste any job description. The app extracts the skills that matter, scores how well your profile matches, and downloads a PDF that leans into the overlap — in about 10 seconds.

Try it live at cv.rewura.com
1

Paste a job description

Drop in the text from any LinkedIn / ATS posting. No account, no import dance — just paste.

2

See your match score

The matcher extracts skills and weighs required-vs-nice-to-have. You get a % score plus a breakdown of matched and missing skills.

3

Download a tailored PDF

The CV template reorders your skills to put the matches first, adds a QR to your portfolio URL, and exports a clean single-page PDF.

Built on FastAPI, Helm, and WeasyPrint

Python backend, WeasyPrint-rendered PDFs, Markdown-driven CV template, and a Helm chart for Kubernetes deployment. Runs equally well locally, on AWS App Runner, or on a managed Kubernetes cluster.
